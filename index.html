About the Event

Youth Parliament 2025 is a national-level simulation of the Indian Parliament hosted by Udai Pratap Autonomous College. Students represent leaders, debate real issues, and sharpen public speaking, policy analysis, and teamwork.

 Oratory  Policy  Leadership  Innovation

Quick Highlights

  • Realistic parliamentary procedure
  • Ruling & Opposition teams (3+3 members)
  • Certificates & merit recognition
  • Venue: UP College Auditorium, Varanasi
